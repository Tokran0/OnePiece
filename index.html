Gear 4 snakeman

The Rubber Fruit, also known as Gomu Gomu no Mi or Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika is one of the Mythical Devil Fruits. In this form your arms are a red color because of the Haki infused in them. In this form, your character has a slightly higher jump and increased run speed. The Rubber Fruit does not have Gear Fourth Snakeman initially. To get Gear Fourth Snakeman, you have to kill Snakeman Luffy (In Wilderness), with a 1% chance of dropping the Gear 4 Snakeman Scroll, once you have it, make sure you have the Rubber Fruit, Gear Second and Gear Fourth then use it. This fruit also has Gear Second, Gear Fourth, and Gear Fifth forms.
